There have always been different breeds of dogs in the family: collie, toy terrier, taxi... there were Persian cats, sphinx, Siamese and Angora cats, there were still rats, mice and hamsters, guinea pigs, parrots and even a hedgehog and dove lived at one time. We always lived together, together with the children we always looked after animals!

At that time, I had only a cat at home and it seemed that something was missing in my soul, namely, I wanted to have a dog. I was going to buy a dog of the toyterrier breed and for the sake of curiosity, I went to look at an unusual breed for me....

This day changed my whole life and I am grateful to fate, that this little girl showed up! And now I can't imagine life without it!

Our History

If you are on our website, then you too are not indifferent to this unique breed - CHINESE CRESTED DOG. Here you will learn more about these amazing dogs!

«Everyone has the dog he deserve»
© German dog handler H. Swarowski

In fact, our nursery Chinese crested dogs exists since 2008, officially registered in 2009. In May 2019, we celebrate the Anniversary of the nursery - we are 10 years old! Over 10 years of tribal work in the nursery, 60 litters (236 puppies) were received.

  17. Also in Estonia, Italy, Brazil.

Our goal is to get healthy adequate dogs, with a stable psyche, trainees and active, meet the requirements of the standard as much as possible. Such a puppy will be for you an ideal friend and companion, a member of the family.

None of our Chinese crested dogs are contained in cages/aviaries or separate rooms. All dogs are full members of our family, communicate closely with other animals and people.
